About the Studio

Built by builders,
for real users.

Loveiko Labs is a lean indie studio based in Thailand, building focused AI-powered tools for the App Store and beyond. We don't chase trends — we solve specific problems with precision software.

Every app we ship starts with a clear user pain and ends with something that actually works. We use AI throughout our stack — from ideation to implementation — letting a small team punch way above its weight.

Our apps reach 20+ markets globally with full localization, and we operate with the speed and conviction of founders who care about craft.
